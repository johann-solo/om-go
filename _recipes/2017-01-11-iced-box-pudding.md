## Ingredients

* 1 tin Nestle cream
* 1 1/4 cup sugar
* 1/2 lb. butter
* 2 tsp. coffee
* 1 tsp. vanilla
* 1 egg
* 4 tbsp. cocoa power
* lady fingersor sponge cake
* Marachino cherries (optional)

## Directions:

1. Cream, butter, sugar, separate egg
1. put yolk in butter and sugar
1. add coffee, vanilla, sifted cocoa powder
1. fold in beaten egg white
